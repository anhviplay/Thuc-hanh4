The Corn Maze

Welcome to our famous Corn Maze! This year's maze is bigger and better than ever, offering an exciting adventure for the whole family. Navigate through towering corn stalks and discover hidden surprises around every corner. Perfect for all ages, our maze provides hours of fun and challenge.

The Corn Maze is open during daylight hours and transforms into our Haunted Maze after dark during the Halloween season. Come early in the day to explore the larger Corn Maze, stay for a homemade meal of brats and burgers, soda, and chips, and then participate in our special events. A smaller maze is also available that is perfect for younger children.

  • Open 11 am - 5 pm on Sundays
  • Weekday visits by appointment
